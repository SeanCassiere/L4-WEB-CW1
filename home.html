What are we?

Detailed Beats was created by its founders for the soul purpose of giving people a way to listen to modern music using more traditional forms of listening, more specifically Vinyl.

Who are we?

Detailed Beats is a small expanding business, run by its founders Sean Cassiere, Isanka Rashmitha, Dinuka Bandara and Kavisha Wijeyasuriya who are passionate about experiencing audiophile grade music.

The founders came up with the idea of Detailed Beats while discussing their passion for good quality music. From then on, they researched and worked to find a solution to make access to good quality music easy for everyone.
